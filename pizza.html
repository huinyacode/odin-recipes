Pizza Mozzarella

    Ingredients:
  • canned washed tomatoes - 800 g
  • salt - 3 quater of tsp
  • Flour - 1 tbsp
  • mozzarella for pizza - 150 g
  • olive oil «extra virgin» - 1 tsp
  • dry pepper pepperoni - 1 pc
    For the dough:
  • flour for pizza - 500 g
  • olive oil - 20 ml
  • sugar - 10 g
  • salt - 5 g
  • dry yeast - 4 g
  • water - 250 ml

STEP-BY-STEP COOKING RECIPE

Step 1.

Prepare a dough of Neapolitan pizza. To do this, first sieve into a large bowl through a fine sieve of 650 g of flour.
Add 7 g of sou-chih yeast and some salt (not more than 10 g).

Step 2.

Add 30 ml of «extra virgin» olive oil. Gradually pour 300 ml of warm water and mix the dough. If it came out fake,
add no more than 20-30 g of flour.

Step 3.

Vigorously sweep the pizza dough (5 min). Roll it into the ball, leave it on the table under the towel for 5-7 min.
Stir another 10 min. then divide into 2 parts. Roll two balls, put them in a bowl, cover with a towel. Allow to stand 40-50 min.

Step 4.

When the dough rises, remember it. Roll or stretch your hands each piece into a thin 30-35 cm diameter circle.
At the same time, leave the edges thicker. Froze the mortar with baking paper. Lay out the dough, sprinkle it with olive oil.
Evenly distributed filling. For example, tomatoes, basil leaves and mozzarella pieces.

Step 5.

Bake a Neapolitan pizza in a maximum heated oven, at 250 C 10-12 min. Take it out of the doo-hover,
sprinkle it with olive oil, and immediately put it on the table.
