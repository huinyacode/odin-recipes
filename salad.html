Caesar Salad

    Ingridients
  • Romaine salad - 160 g
  • Chicken (fillet) - 200 g
  • Cherry tomatoes - 100 g
  • Parmesan cheese grated - 20 g
  • Toast - 2 handfuls
  • Olive oil - 4 tsp
  • Salt, freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Dry white wine
    For sauce:
  • Mayonnaise - 150 g
  • Natural yoghurt - 150 g
  • Capers - 20 g
  • Dijon mustard - 20 g
  • Anchovy (fillet) - 100 g

STEP-BY-STEP COOKING RECIPE

Step 1.

Prepare the ingredients of the salad. Rinse the chicken fillet, cut with small bars, salt, pepper and fry on olive oil,
slowly adding white wine.

Step 2.

For salad sauce mix all the ingredients in the blender. Note: the sauce recipe is given for 10 servings.
For 2 servings of salad requires 6 art. l. sauce. The finished sauce can be stored in the fridge for about a week at a temperature of 4C.

Step 3.

Wash the lettuce leaves with running water, then dry thoroughly with paper towels. Rip it into little
pieces and fill it with the sauce you made earlier.

Step 4.

Add pieces of chicken and stir. Put on a plate or in a salad bowl. Wash the tomatoes and cut them in half.
Put it on the salad with the toast and cheese.
