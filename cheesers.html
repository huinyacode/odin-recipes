Cheesecake

    Ingredients:
  • cottage cheese - 350 g
  • flour - 150 g
  • egg - 1 pc
  • sugar - 60 g
  • oil - 2 spoons
  • butter - 30 g
  • vanilla sugar - 1 tea spoon
  • salt - quater of tea spoon

STEP-BY-STEP COOKING RECIPE

Step 1.

Put the curd in a large bowl. Best for cheeses suitable curd with a fat content of 5-9%. Crumbled and
dry cottage cheese is better to break through with a blender, so that in the cheeses there are no particles of undisturbed curd.

Step 2.

If the curd is soft and plastic, you can not use a blender. This quality is usually matched with fat plastic curd.
If desired, you can make cheesecake and skim cheese.

Step 3.

Pour 100 g of flour into a bowl of cottage cheese and add a small pinch of salt. Stir the curd dough thoroughly
until it is homogeneous with a silicone shovel or fork.

Step 4.

In a separate dish, combine the egg with ordinary and vanilla sugar. Beat the mixer into a thick light foam.
Pour the whipped egg into a bowl of curd dough and gently stir it with a spatula.

Step 5.

Cover the bowl with food film and leave on the working surface for 5 minutes. During this time, the dough
will become elastic and malleable, it will be easy to form Cheesecakes.

Step 6.

Pour the rest of the flour into the plate. Use your wet hands to separate a piece of dough the size of a table tennis ball.
Give it a round shape, roll it in flour and put it on a clean plate or on a board. Continue to form round cheeses until
the dough is finished.

Step 7.

In a large frying pan, heat the vegetable oil with medium heat. Reduce the heating to a minimum and add butter.
Each prepared cheese slightly press your hands, shaping the washer, put on the pan and cover the lid.

Step 8.

Prepare the cheesecake for 4-5 minutes, then flip to the other side and continue cooking under the lid for another 3 minutes.
Remove the lid and bring the cheese to the ready, 1 min. Serve hot with sour cream, honey and berries.
